    How does a Quiz Bowl work?

    What is Quizbowl?

    Quizbowl is a knowledge based competition with questions from many different fields.

    How it Works?

    The first person to buzz in gets to answer. If he answers right, he can get 10-15 points based on speed.

    Why Quizbowl?

    Quizbowl provides opportunities for everyone of every age. No matter who you are, you will find a home here.

    Who can Come?

    Quizbowl questions are written in 3 different grade levels: Elementary, Junior, and Senior.

    Question Copyright?

    There is none! You can use Quizbowl questions anywhere for your personal uses!

    Where to Practice?

    You can always practice in live communication like Zoom, or go to buzzin.live, but it is recommended to buy a buzzer system.

    How to Make Team?

    First find some teammates. Then contact a faculty member. Get in touch with other teams and the LIQBA. A faculty member will show you how to get administrator approval. Then practice!

    Buzzer Systems

    The cheapest approved buzzer system is priced at $290 for 10 and $305 to get the included bag. Find it here

    Useful Tricks

    Acquire correct and right study materials, decide what formats to play, and master the buzz-in system.

    Game Play Rules

    In a conventional round of quizbowl, there will be 20 question cycles a game. A question cycle is composed of a single tossup question and three shorter bonus questions.

    What are Toss Ups and bonuses?

    Tossups are paragraph long questions players can buzz in at any time to answer. If a player answers a tossup correctly, their team can get 3 bonus questions.

    When do Quiz Bowls Tournaments happen?

    Tournaments are usually an all-day affair, running from the morning to late in the afternoon.
